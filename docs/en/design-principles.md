# Design Principles

These principles guide the conceptual direction of OceanRithm.

## 1. Abstraction First Thinking
Focus on ideas and models rather than implementation.

## 2. Governance Oriented Design
Workflows are viewed through a conceptual governance lens.

## 3. Modular Conceptual Layers
Each layer behaves independently at the idea level.

## 4. Research Reproducibility
Designed to support academic investigation.

## 5. Conceptual Multi Tenancy
Tenant separation is theoretical no technical methods are disclosed.
